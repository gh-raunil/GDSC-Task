Meet Our Speakers

1️⃣ Dr. Sumit Saurav

🎓 AI & ML Expert | Professor at IIT Bombay

🔹 Dr. Sumit is a leading AI researcher with 20+ years of expertise in Machine Learning and Deep Learning. He has contributed to AI startups, published 50+ research papers, and delivered keynote speeches at global conferences, shaping discussions on AI innovation, ethics, and real-world applications.

2️⃣ Kajal Rituraj

💻 Senior Software Engineer at Google | Web Development Specialist

🔹 Kajal has been working with Google for 7+ years, specializing in frontend technologies like React, Angular, and Web Performance Optimization. She has mentored thousands of developers through Google’s Women Techmakers program and is passionate about making web technologies accessible to everyone.

3️⃣ Shubham Kumar

🚀 Co-founder of DevX | Cloud Architect

🔹 Shubham is a cloud solutions architect with expertise in AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes. He co-founded DevX, a platform helping developers scale applications efficiently. He has conducted numerous workshops on cloud-native development and is known for his engaging, hands-on teaching approach.
